The Applied Side

Program evaluation is where the methods meet the field: process and outcome evaluations, assessment tools, and research briefs for courts and justice agencies, at the National Center for State Courts and before that directing research for the Juvenile Justice Institute's Evidence-based Nebraska project. Most of these reports were published to agency sites that have since moved or retired them, so titles are listed here and copies are available on request.
