Where this comes from. The formats and their reach, length, and illustration-to-text ratio are drawn from the delivery-formats tree (Figure 2) in Lindsey Wylie's "Cultivating a Court's Data STORY," NCSC Trends in State Courts (2025). The audience subgroups (novice through funder) are that chapter's Figure 3. The mapping of which format suits which audience and objective is a starting point meant to be argued with, not a verdict.
