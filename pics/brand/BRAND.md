# Data with a Plot — Brand Sheet
Lindsey E. Wylie, J.D., Ph.D. · datawithaplot.org

## Identity
A fine-nib fountain pen draws a narrative arc through a scatter of data points: the data is real and messy, the story is fitted through it. Tone: scholarly with a wink — refined, precise, quietly funny ("n = 1 website. Results may not generalize.").

## Color palette (accessible pairings)
| Token | Hex | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Ink Plum | #2E1A47 | primary dark: backgrounds, headings, body on light |
| Cream | #F7F2EC | primary light: backgrounds, text on dark (13.8:1 AAA vs Ink Plum) |
| Paper | #FDFBF7 | lighter surface for cards/panels on Cream |
| Coral | #F4715C | accent ON DARK only for text (5.1:1 AA vs Ink Plum); decorative anywhere |
| Deep Coral | #B23A28 | accent for text/links ON LIGHT (5.4:1 AA vs Cream) |
| Mauve | #A98BB8 | secondary, decorative only — never body text |

Rules: max 1–2 background colors per artifact. Coral text never sits on Cream (2.7:1 — fails); use Deep Coral instead. Mauve is for dots, rules, and labels ≥14px bold at most.

## Typography
- **Spectral** (Google Fonts) — everything: headings 600, body 400, italic 400 for emphasis and the "with a" in the wordmark. Fallback: Georgia, serif.
- **JetBrains Mono** — eyebrows, captions, data labels, code. 11–13px, letter-spacing 0.08–0.12em for eyebrows. Fallback: monospace.

## Logo
Wordmark: "Data *with a* Plot" — Spectral 600; "with a" italic 400 in Coral (on dark) / Deep Coral (on light).
Files (SVG, in /assets):
- logo-horizontal-on-dark.svg / logo-horizontal-on-light.svg
- logo-stacked-on-dark.svg / logo-stacked-on-light.svg
- mark-on-dark.svg / mark-on-light.svg (icon only)
- favicon.svg (ink rounded square, simplified mark — drop detail below 32px)
Clear space: half the mark's height on all sides. Don't rotate, recolor outside the palette, or add axes to the arc.

## Graphic motifs
- **The arc**: one smooth rise-and-fall curve, stroke 3–5px, round caps. No x/y axes.
- **The scatter**: 4–7 mauve dots loosely around the arc; exactly one coral dot (the outlier / the climax — the one the line misses).
- **The pen**: line-art fountain pen at 45°, tip touching the arc's end. Omit below ~40px.
- Dashed lines use round dots (stroke-dasharray "1 8"), mauve.

## Voice
Bright lines vs. the people in between. Statistical humor welcome, sparingly. Title case headings, sentence case body; eyebrows in mono caps. No em dashes (use colons or commas).
